AWS Auto-scaling Notes

May 19 2016

Auto Scaling helps you ensure that you have the correct number of EC2 instances available to handle the load for your application. You create collections of EC2 instances, called Auto Scaling groups.

Auto Scaling Components

  1. Groups:

    Your EC2 instances are originzed into groups.

  2. Launch configurations:

    Your group uses a launch configuration as a template for its EC2 instances. When you create a launch configuration, you can specify information such as the AMI ID, instance type, security groups, and block device mapping for your instances.

  3. Scaling plans:

    A scaling plan tells Auto Scaling when and how to scale.
